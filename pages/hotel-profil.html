ABOUT THE HOTEL

Each night, around 6pm, a kaleidoscope of color erupts in the sky above Bali as the sun sets on another perfect day in paradise. The breathtaking cliff-top locale where The Villas at AYANA Resort, BALI resides provides the perfect vantage point.

There’s a tranquil island spirit at play in this secluded locale, making it the perfect venue for a honeymoon or an intimate haven to relax. There are just 78 spacious private pool villas within, each boasting luxurious linens, indigenous artworks and oversized marble bathrooms. No detail is too small for the resort’s roster of internationally certified butlers, on-hand 24-hours to ensure the utmost in comfort.

The Villas at AYANA Resort, BALI sprawls over 90-hectares above the Indian Ocean, with a full spectrum of luxurious amenities to sample. There are 19 unique restaurant, bar and entertainment options, catering to all tastes and ages. There’s also the small matter of the 12 magnificent swimming pools on-site, and an 18-hole putting course that will let aspiring Jordan Speith’s keep their putter hot. Those traveling with children will also be delighted to hear of the presence of a daily kids' club.

Guests can pamper mind, body and soul at Thermes Marins Bali Spa at AYANA Resort. Signature treatments, such as Aquatonic Seawater Therapy in one of the world’s largest Aquatonic Pools or the innovative AYANA Fusion treatment will provide overall relaxation. Those in the mood to elevate their spa experience to the next level can sample an indulgent treatment in one of our private seaside gazebos at Spa on The Rocks.
