BEACH HOTELS

Welcome on the sandy white beaches.
How could anyone resist a locale with a moniker as charming as the Seychelles? Off the coast of Africa, and surrounded by the deep waters of the Indian Ocean, Constance Lemuria Seychelles makes a secluded, relaxing destination for both man, woman, and sea turtle.
So much so for the turtles, that they choose to lay their eggs here. If guests are careful and respectful, they can watch the entire delicate process unfold.
