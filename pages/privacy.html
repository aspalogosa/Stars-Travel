PRIVACY POLICY


Is to hold your data securely, Share only where you agree, Use your data to tailor the information we provide you, arrange your travel arrangements and improve our services, Put you in control by allowing you to update, delete and access your data.We are implementing and maintaining appropriate technical and organisational security measures, policies and procedures designed to protect the personal information that you share with us and safeguard the privacy of such information. You have a right to request that we provide you with a copy of your personal information that we hold and you have the right to be informed of: the source of your personal information; the purposes, legal basis and methods of processing; the data controller’s identity; and the businesses or categories of businesses to whom your personal information may be transferred.


Data protection

Thank you for visiting our website and for your interest in our company and the services we offer. We respect your privacy and ensure the protection of your personal data by processing your personal data in accordance with the contents of these data protection regulations and the applicable data protection laws.

You can visit our website without needing to identify yourself to us. In order to be able to display our websites, you are only obliged to provide the data transmitted by your browser to our server (see "Logfiles"). Further personal data will only be stored if you voluntarily enter it on the website or use corresponding functions, e.g. when registering for our newsletter.


Bookings and orders, customer account

In order to make a booking or order, you must enter the data requested in the corresponding form as mandatory data. We process this data for the purpose of carrying out your order and for the required communication with you (Art. 6 Para. 1 (1) (b) GDPR). A telephone number is important for the order process in case there are any questions about the order. You can create a customer account with us in order to simplify future bookings or orders. In this case, we store this data for you for the purpose of facilitating future bookings or orders. You can have your user account deleted at any time by sending an e-mail to us at the contact details listed under "Data controller". Otherwise, we will delete your data with the complete processing of the contract after expiry of the retention periods under tax and commercial law.


Newsletter

If you subscribe to our electronic newsletter, you consent to the storage of your name and e-mail address for the purpose of sending the newsletter (Art. 6 Para. 1 (1) (a) GDPR). This data will not be passed on to third parties. You can at any time revoke your newsletter consent for the future and thus terminate your newsletter subscription (note in the newsletter).


Cookies

Cookies are very small text files used by Internet pages, which your browser stores on your computer and which provide certain information from the place where the cookie is set (here by us). Cookies cannot run programs or transmit viruses to your computer.


Your rights

You have the following rights with us regarding your personal data:

  • Right to information
  • Right to amendment or deletion
  • Right to limitation of processing
  • Right of opposition to processing
  • Right to data transferability.

You also have the right to complain to a data protection supervisory authority about our processing of your personal data.
